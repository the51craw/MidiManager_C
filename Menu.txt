# DX7/Juno-106/AUX 1/AUX 2/Drumpads
* Channel/Transpose+/Transpose-

Press # for cycling inputs.
Press * for toggling Channel/Tranpose.
Press digits to enter value, changes immediately.
Values are shown at screen right.

Examples:


JUNO-106      0_
Channel      16

DX7         -12
Channel      16_

When a value is entered outside limits, the limit value is saved.

Limits:
Transpose +/- 24 semitones.
Channel 1 - 16.

Buttons: 

Synt +/-
Channel +/-
Transpose +/-
